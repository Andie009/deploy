Edible vs Poisonous Mushrooms in dataset

It is split approximately 50:50 of edible and poisonous mushrooms in the dataset

Cap Shapes

Mushrooms with Sunken and Conical cap shapes are always edible

Cap Colour

Mushrooms with Purple and Green caps are always edible

Odor

Mushrooms with Foul, Fishy, Spicy, Pungent, Creosote and Musty odors are always poisonous. Mushrooms with Anise and Almond odors are always edible.
