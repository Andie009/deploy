About the Mushroom Classification Project

My inspiration for this project was from a trip to Italy. I was hiking in the Piane delle Mele mountains of Abruzzo when I noticed people foraging for mushrooms. I thought to myself how do they know which is safe to eat and which are poisonous? After doing a bit of research, foraging for mushrooms is quite a popular hobby and is colloquially known as "shrooming". I found this mushroom dataset and thought I would test and train it and see if I could build a model and an app for users to input characteristics they find on a mushroom and to help them determine if it was edible or poisonous.
